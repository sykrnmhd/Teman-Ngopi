About Us

Background


We are a crew that started out small, just like any other business. One of our founding members came up with the idea to create a game-changing coffee shop. Ever since, we aim to give you only the best coffee in town.

Vision and Mission


We strive to ensure that our services are tiptop whenever it comes to serving our products to our customers. However, we do not forget the environment while doing so. Our target is to be 100% environmental-friendly and inspire other businesses to join us in the cause as well.

Sustainable Implementation


We use recycleable material for our product packaging to ensure the safety of our environment. We only provide paper straw on demand from customers. Our packaging is fully made from biodegredable material, so it will not be a waste and threat to the nature.
